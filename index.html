Latest Project

E-Jantaa

E-Jantaa is a user-friendly platform designed to empower communities by facilitating the reporting and resolution of local issues. With E-Jantaa, residents can easily report various concerns, such as potholes, broken streetlights, or waste management problems, directly to local authorities. The platform fosters transparency and accountability by allowing users to track the progress of reported issues and participate in prioritizing problem-solving efforts through voting mechanisms. E-Jantaa aims to create a more responsive and connected community, where every voice matters in building a better neighborhood. Github Link
